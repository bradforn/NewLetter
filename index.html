An Issue with Textbooks Pricing

The cost of textbooks over the last couple of scores has grown out of control. It is one of the most complained about issues among students and for good reason too. Student debt has become a familiar topic amount economics across the nation. SPRC has decided to focus reducing the costs of textbooks for students at Oregon State University. It is our belief that reducing the cost of textbook prices will result in reducing the burden of financing college.
